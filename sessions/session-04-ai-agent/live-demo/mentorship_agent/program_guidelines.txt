--- MENTORSHIP PROGRAM GUIDELINES & ELIGIBILITY ---

MENTOR ELIGIBILITY

Experience: Must have 3+ years of professional experience in their field.
Availability: Must commit to at least 2 hours per month.
Verification: Must provide a valid LinkedIn or GitHub URL that matches their application details.
Role: Must currently hold a Senior, Lead, or Management role, OR demonstrate significant specialized expertise.

MENTEE ELIGIBILITY
Experience: Must have 1+ year of professional experience in their field.
Goals: Must articulate 2-3 specific learning goals (e.g., "Learn System Design", "Transition to PM").
Availability: Must commit to 1 hour per month for sessions + 2 hours for homework.
Status: Open to all levels, including students and career switchers.

MATCHING CRITERIA
Skills must strictly match (e.g., do not match a Python Mentee with a Java Mentor unless the Mentor also knows Python).
Time zones should be within 4 hours overlap unless both parties agree to async mentorship.
